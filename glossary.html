meaningful gift #mega

A gift that is provided by a meaningful gift giver that is made with magic and delivered with love based not on any business-transaction, but on verifiable proof of the recipient's NAUGHTY or NICE credentials. Meaningful gift recipients must be humans who qualify as children in the local jurisdiction.

version 2, commit 25ba51f, created 2021-12-09, last modified 2021-12-09, contributors Nicky Hickman

meaningful gift arrangement #mega

In a jurisdiction: the specification of a set of rights and duties between legal entities of the jurisdiction that enforces these rights and duties, for the purpose of giving meaningful gifts to one or more of these entities.

version 1, commit b24a5b3, created 2021-12-09, contributors Nicky Hickman

meaningful gift giver #mega

A party who has either magical or spiritual rights and duties to deliver meaningful gifts to humans designated as children in their jurisdiction.

version 1, commit 847d7ab, created 2021-12-09, contributors Nicky Hickman

meaningful gift occasion #mega

An 24 hour period in the life of a human child either linked to lunar cycles (e.g. winter solstice), life events (e.g. losing a milk tooth), or rites of passage (e.g. naming ceremony).
